AcoustiSense Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how AcoustiSense collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Introduction

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide: This includes information you submit when registering, contacting us, or using our services.
  • Usage data: We automatically collect information about how you use our services, such as the pages you visit and the features you use.
  • Device information: We may collect information about your device, such as your IP address and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Communicate with you about our services.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. Please contact us at support@acoustisense.com to exercise these rights.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@acoustisense.com.
